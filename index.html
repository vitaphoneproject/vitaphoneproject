The Vitaphone Soundtrack Project

The Vitaphone Soundtrack Project seeks to preserve, digitize, restore and make freely available online the surviving soundtracks from the earliest years of talking pictures (1926–1931), especially from films that are otherwise lost.

By preserving Vitaphone discs and surviving soundtrack recordings, we work to reunite sound with surviving film elements whenever possible and make these restorations freely available for everyone to enjoy.

Help Save Lost Soundtracks

Top dollar paid for Vitaphone discs and early feature film soundtracks (1926–1931). Donations are also gratefully accepted.

We can provide safe packing materials for fragile shellac Vitaphone discs. Donors may receive restoration credit and digital copies when possible.
