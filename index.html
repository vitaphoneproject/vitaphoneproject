About The Vitaphone Project

The Vitaphone Project is dedicated to the preservation, restoration, and documentation of early sound motion pictures and their surviving sound recordings.

Through research, restoration efforts, and collaboration with archives, collectors, historians, and preservation organizations, we work to protect the legacy of the pioneering era of talking pictures.
