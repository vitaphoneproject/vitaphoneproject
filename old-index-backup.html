The Vitaphone Soundtrack Project

Preserving And Restoring Early Sound Films

Dedicated to the restoration, preservation, and documentation of early sound motion pictures. This is a non-profit effort to digitize soundtracks and films from The Early Sound Period (1926-1931) and make them available freely online.

About

The Vitaphone Project is dedicated to saving the history of early sound cinema and restoring important films and recordings for future generations.
